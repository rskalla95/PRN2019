Assessments

The PRN has assessments which can target the following populations:

  • Individual Adult- Adults attending therapy alone
  • Couple- Attending with their partner in a committed relationship
  • Parent- For parents attending with children
  • Individual Adolescent- Attending individually or with a parent

The 40+ assessments cover areas related to client well-being including anxiety, parenting, sexual satisfaction, attachment, emotional regulation, violence, finances, and many other areas.

In developing the PRN assessments, both reliability and validity were top priorities while also keeping the questionnaire length short and manageable for clients. These questionnaires are designed to be used in a clinical setting and allow the therapist to see their clients' scores in comparison to the clinical cut-off and the population norm.
